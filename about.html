ABOUT BEQA ISLAND RESORT

Rated one of the best resorts in the world by Scuba Diving Magazine readers for quality, rooms, restaurant, staff, and value.

Beqa Lagoon Resort is highly regarded for its world-class shark diving, genuine hospitality, and relaxing atmosphere, with many guests calling it an "instant family" or "paradise". The resort is famous for the "Cathedral" shark dive, the luxurious Lali Spa, and the authentic, welcoming staff

The island of Beqa, which is most known for its spectacular lagoon, was named the "Soft Coral Capital of the World" by the famous oceanographer Jacques Cousteau. Renowned for its world-class diving, Beqa Lagoon is a protected UNESCO biosphere reserve that is home to a teeming array of marine life.

A few minutes away from Fiji's Pacific Harbour, Beqa offers travellers with world-class diving and luxury resorts - browse all-inclusive holiday deals here!
